*Revere XR · RVR Immersive Storytelling Hackathon · 2024 · my first hackathon.*

A two-day immersive-storytelling hackathon focused on **cultural-history preservation**. My
team, **Continuum XR** — "because we were building a continuum from the past into the future" —
designed an **AR community space at the former Blackbird bookstore**, a site of great
geo-cultural value that's now a parking lot. The concept grew directly out of Ms. Virginia
Wyman's stories of her upbringing and her family's work toward racial equality in Seattle.
