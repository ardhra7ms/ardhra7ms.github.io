*Ather Energy · People Communications & Employer Branding · 2021–2022.*
*Draft built from my portfolio deck — fuller story and reflections to come.*

## The brief
A website that portrays Ather's culture — beyond an application portal, something
more immersive. A real representation of people, diversity, and teams, with an
improved application process and a hunt for other pain points along the way.

## Messaging
- People make the brand
- Industry leaders
- Collaborative work
- An EV producer with many industry-firsts (even at a global level): revolutionary
  design, performance, software tech, and people strategy & culture

## Challenges
- Multiple goals and stakeholders: Marketing, Employer Branding, HR
- Large-scale asset creation: brand + user
- Strict timelines and a cross-functional swimlane

## Process
Research (business goals, perspectives of functional leaders, competitive analysis,
voice and tone of the brand) → understand applicants' needs and career paths →
define voice (aspirational, authentic, engaging) → practice ("start with the word,"
the New Joiners' handbook) → IA + wireframing + drafting → feedback and iteration
(early notes: "verbose, generic") → landing-page ideation → UAT with a new batch of
employees → optimize for readability and information gaps. Technical roadblocks
along the way: JD tooling, plugins, being short-handed.

## Outcomes
- **Usability** — met messaging goals; improved site navigation and process for
  both applicants and recruiters
- **Accessibility** — responsive, screenreader-friendly, with guide texts
- **Efficiency** — more applications, faster JD uploads, better screening
- **Reception & pride** — meaningful CTAs
- The website is still in place after 3+ years → [careers.atherenergy.com](https://careers.atherenergy.com/)
