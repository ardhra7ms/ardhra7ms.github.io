*Neur XR Studios · Head of Content Development, founding team · 2020–2021.*
*Draft built from my portfolio deck — fuller story and reflections to come.*

Neur XR Studios is an ambitious Extended Reality startup in India.

## Goals
- Enhance the textbook learning experience (a pandemic-era need)
- Leverage immersive technology
- Build for accessibility and inclusion

## Challenges
- The pandemic
- Budget & resources
- Limited subject expertise & user research
- Audio content
- Managing a team
- A long product development cycle (a full year)

## Process
Research (market needs, textbook limitations, AR's capabilities, audio content) →
ideate (design system and elements, gamification, engaging multiple senses) →
prototype & iterate (feedback to reduce elements and improve usability).

*Design for one, design for all.*

## Outcomes
- A working product for market testing — with very limited resources
- Established a content team and system
- Established a product development cycle
- Improved accessibility
