*Assessment project · accessibility and localization.*
*Draft built from my portfolio deck — fuller story and reflections to come.*

## Goal
Assess the accessibility and localization of the Nike Careers experience.

## Process
- Test and detect accessibility issues
- Conduct user research and analyze the results
- Redesign based on pain points and some corner cases
- Translation assessment for one language (Hindi)

## Challenges
- It was already a good website
- Finding job applicants with overlooked accessibility needs

## Issues detected
- A very long application
- "Find your fit" was buried
- Contrast issues on some pages, including the landing page
- Long, unreadable texts
- Some English words not translated
- Screenreader misses sub-headers & vertical text

## Other findings
- Buttons are responsive to translation — wordy but clear
- Broken links
- Suspected accidental tone alignment of the translation (very aspirational)

## Learning outcomes
- User research methodologies
- Accessibility tools and checks
- Experience design is never finished
