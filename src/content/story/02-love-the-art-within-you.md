---

I have always had an urge to draw, colour, play with clay, make things with my hand. Somewhere along the way I lost touch with that side of myself a little but, turns out it's a way I ground myself in moments of stress, just letting my hand find its way out of the mess my brain conjures. I love that it's so accessible and powerful. **We can change our mindset whenever, wherever, even if only one stroke or tune at a time.**

While art and creativity are parts of my being, people and relationships are central parts too. Looking back, it was natural for me to lean towards Communications and Ux because of my tendency to enjoy working with people, learn and be inspired by them, and be of use to purposes that feel bigger than myself. That's important to me, to follow purpose.
