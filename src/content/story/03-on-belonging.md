I've learnt that all communities has innies and outies, and it blows my mind how many ways people find to make themselves seem part of a group, or not seem part of a group they don't consider cool. I also love that people have invented the arts and languages to an infinite depth that is comparable to the depth of the mysteries of the universe. **That one person holds an infinity within themselves is a fact that's both reassuring and terrifying.**

Another interesting thing is there is almost nothing that is universal, what works with one person or community can't always work with another, and design needs to be more dynamic and adaptive to people's needs than it is today, and I think AI can really help in creating dynamic tools to build customized UX. I'm not a fan of gathering all user data in order to do this, rather I want for products to give users choice in what kind of experience they get.
