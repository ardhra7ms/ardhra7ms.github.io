The underlying thread between wriing, designing, and engineering, is that they are **different ways of looking at problems, but they're all about solving and making sense or meaning of things.**

To me, these are not disjointed ideas or fields - writing helps me reflect and make sense of my world and the world around me, design helps put that into some kind of form, and engineering is the expression, which is the act of building systems and getting them to work. By no means do I claim to be a genius coder though, that's something I am not. :P
